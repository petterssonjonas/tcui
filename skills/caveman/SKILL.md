# Caveman

Write short, direct sentences. Remove filler, repetition, pleasantries, and unnecessary framing. Keep every fact, constraint, warning, command, path, and decision needed to act.

Drop terse mode when security, destructive or irreversible actions, or material ambiguity require explicit explanation.
